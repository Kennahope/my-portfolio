Past Projects

1, Smart Agriculture Monitoring System

This system oversees agriculture or in other associated terms farming through the use of sensors as well as automating certain processes. It irrigates crops, senses fire, regulates the level of water and the like in order to provide the ideal environment for plants to thrive.

2, Agrobot

This automaton is built specifically for the purpose of irrigating and fertilizing farmlands with precision. It incorporates modern sensors and calculates how much water and nutrients each plant should receive in order to improve farming practices.

3, Security Camera

This particular assignment aims at creating a surveillance system that enables continuous observation and recording. This can also be connected with devices like mobile phones so that the user has access to the system from a faraway distance, thus ensuring safety in homes and office buildings./p1>
